Projects

Afri-Intelligence Web Application

Afri-Intelligence is a digital learning platform designed to support students and teachers across Africa by making education more accessible, personalized, and engaging. The platform allows students to register and log in, access past exam papers, use text-to-speech tools for better comprehension, and explore a terms glossary to strengthen subject understanding. Teachers can also register to upload learning materials and support students directly. A key feature of Afri-Intelligence is its AI-driven assistant, tailored to the African education context, which helps students with explanations, study support, and personalized learning recommendations.
